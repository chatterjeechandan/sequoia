3 Reasons why Sequoia products are the preffered supplement brand

Cutting-Edge Liposome Technology

Liposomes are tiny, spherical structures that encapsulate nutrients, protecting them as they pass through the digestive system and delivering them directly to your cells. This technology enhances the bioavailability of our products, meaning your body can absorb and utilize the nutrients more efficiently. With Sequoia, you get the maximum benefit from every dose, supporting your health at a cellular level.

Certified by the Ministry of Health

We are proud to be certified by the Israel Ministry of Health, a testament to our commitment to quality and safety. This certification ensures that our products meet stringent health and safety standards, giving you peace of mind that you are consuming supplements that are both effective and trustworthy.

Great Tasting Liquid Supplements

Sequoia supplements are not only effective but also enjoyable to take. Our great-tasting liquid formulas make it easier for everyone to incorporate essential nutrients into their daily routine. The liquid form is particularly beneficial for children or individuals who have difficulty swallowing pills.
