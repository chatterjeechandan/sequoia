Our R&D lab at Technion University is where science meets innovation. Our researchers and scientists collaborate to explore new ways to enhance the efficacy of our supplements through advanced liposomal technology. This state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest technology, allowing us to conduct thorough research and develop products that meet the highest standards of quality and effectiveness.

Adhering to
Strict Guidelines

Quality and safety are paramount at Sequoia. We adhere to the stringent guidelines set forth by the Israel Ministry of Health, ensuring that every product we develop meets rigorous health and safety standards. Our commitment to these standards is reflected in our ISO certification, which signifies our dedication to maintaining excellence in every aspect of our production process.

ISO 9001-2015
and Ministry
of Health
Certification

Sequoia proudly holds ISO certification, a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality management and continuous improvement. This certification ensures that our processes are efficient, reliable, and consistently meet regulatory requirements. Additionally, our products are approved by the Israel Ministry of Health, providing further assurance of their safety and efficacy.
